# Warlock Perk Trees

## Overview
Warlocks are masters of forbidden knowledge who have made pacts with otherworldly beings of immense power. They channel their patron's dark gifts through their signature ability—Eldritch Blast—while expanding their magical versatility through arcane invocations and esoteric knowledge. Warlocks grow more powerful through their patron relationship, perfecting their arcane craft and bending reality to their will.

**Class Focus:** Warlocks excel at:
- Casting Eldritch Blast with powerful invocations and devastating forms
- Managing patron-granted abilities and dark one's blessings
- Versatile spellcasting through pact bonuses and spell recovery
- Manipulating reality through eldritch invocations and forbidden knowledge

**Total Design Budget:** 120 points worth of perks (for 90 perk points earned)

## Tree 1: Eldritch Mastery (48 points total)
*Master the quintessential warlock ability—Eldritch Blast—unlocking devastating new forms and terrible power*

### Tier 1 Perks (1 point each)

1. **Agonizing Blast I**
   - Cost: 1 point per rank
   - Max Rank: 3
   - Effect: +1 damage per rank to Eldritch Blast (stacks with Charisma modifier)
   - Prerequisite: None
   - Notes: Core warlock ability enhancement, allows Eldritch Blast to deal meaningful damage

2. **Critical Conduit I**
   - Cost: 1 point per rank
   - Max Rank: 3
   - Effect: +1% critical hit chance per rank for Eldritch Blast
   - Prerequisite: None
   - Notes: Improves burst reliability without relying on range mechanics

3. **Eldritch Accuracy**
   - Cost: 1 point per rank
   - Max Rank: 3
   - Effect: +1 to hit bonus per rank for Eldritch Blast
   - Prerequisite: None
   - Notes: Represents refined control and targeting

4. **Blast Resilience I**
   - Cost: 1 point per rank
   - Max Rank: 2
   - Effect: When hitting with Eldritch Blast, gain +5 temporary damage resistance to spell damage per rank for 5 rounds.
   - Prerequisite: None
   - Notes: Synergizes with common pact warlock scenarios

---

### Tier 2 Perks (2 points each)

5. **Agonizing Blast II**
   - Cost: 2 points per rank
   - Max Rank: 2
   - Effect: +2 damage per rank to Eldritch Blast, bonus stacks with Charisma Enhancement
   - Prerequisite: Agonizing Blast I (max)
   - Notes: Total of +7 damage possible with all tiers

6. **Critical Conduit II**
   - Cost: 2 points per rank
   - Max Rank: 2
   - Effect: Additional +2% critical hit chance per rank for Eldritch Blast
   - Prerequisite: Critical Conduit I (max)
   - Notes: Total of +7% Eldritch Blast critical chance with both tiers maxed

7. **Cone Blast**
   - Cost: 2 points
   - Max Rank: 1
   - Effect: +10% damage to eldritch blast damage when using eldritch cone
   - Prerequisite: Eldritch Accuracy (2 ranks)
   - Notes: AoE variant of Eldritch Blast

8. **Repelling Blast**
   - Cost: 2 points per rank
   - Max Rank: 2
   - Effect: Each rank grants 5% chance to knock down opponent when using eldritch blast
   - Prerequisite: Critical Conduit I (2 ranks)
   - Notes: Tactical repositioning ability, grants battlefield control

---

### Tier 3 Perks (3-4 points each)

9. **Agonizing Blast III**
   - Cost: 3 points
   - Max Rank: 1
   - Effect: +3 damage to Eldritch Blast, scales with warlock level
   - Prerequisite: Agonizing Blast II (max)
   - Notes: Total of +10 bonus damage possible with all tiers

10. **Chain Blast**
    - Cost: 3 points
    - Max Rank: 1
    - Effect: +10% damage with eldritch blast when using eldritch chain
    - Prerequisite: Cone Blast
    - Notes: Bouncing projectile variant, utility against grouped enemies

11. **Blast Resilience II**
    - Cost: 3 points per rank
    - Max Rank: 2
    - Effect: When hitting with Eldritch Blast, gain additional +5 temporary damage resistance to spell damage per rank for 5 rounds. stacks separately
    - Prerequisite: Blast Resilience I (max)
    - Notes: Significant magical defense enhancement

12. **Devastating Blast**
    - Cost: 4 points
    - Max Rank: 1
    - Effect: Critical hits with Eldritch Blast deal 3d6 bonus damage (instead of 2d6)
    - Prerequisite: Agonizing Blast II (max), Eldritch Accuracy (max)
    - Notes: Increases burst potential on lucky rolls

---

### Tier 4 Capstone Perks (5+ points each)

13. **Eldritch Annihilation**
    - Cost: 5 points
    - Max Rank: 1
    - Effect: +5 damage to Eldritch Blast, Additional +10% damage when using eldritch cone or chain.
    - Prerequisite: Agonizing Blast III, Chain Blast
    - Notes: Ultimate blast damage specialization

14. **Blast Mastery**
    - Cost: 5 points
    - Max Rank: 1
    - Effect: Eldritch Blast ignores 50% of enemy damage resistance
    - Prerequisite: Devastating Blast, Eldritch Accuracy (max)
    - Notes: Supernal accuracy and piercing magical defenses

15. **Reality-Rending Blast**
    - Cost: 6 points
    - Max Rank: 1
    - Effect: 10% chance per Eldritch Blast to deal 2d10 additional force damage that ignores resistances
    - Prerequisite: Eldritch Annihilation, Blast Mastery
    - Notes: Represents tearing at the fabric of reality

16. **Eternal Barrage**
    - Cost: 5 points
    - Max Rank: 1
    - Effect: 5% chance to fire an additional eldritch blast for free when making an eldritch blast
   - Prerequisite: Critical Conduit II (max), Blast Mastery
    - Notes: Doubles theoretical Eldritch Blast output once per action

## Tree 2: Pact Bonding (42 points total)
*Deepen your connection to your patron, unlocking supernatural abilities and dark blessings*

### Tier 1 Perks (1 point each)

1. **Dark One's Blessing I**
   - Cost: 1 point per rank
   - Max Rank: 3
   - Effect: When you reduce a hostile creature to 0 HP, gain temporary HP = (Charisma modifier + warlock level/2) × rank
   - Prerequisite: None
   - Notes: Healing/defensive ability after combat success

2. **Pact Connection**
   - Cost: 1 point per rank
   - Max Rank: 3
   - Effect: +1 spell save DC per rank, representing patron's blessing
   - Prerequisite: None
   - Notes: Baseline spell effectiveness enhancement

3. **Eldritch Sight**
   - Cost: 1 point per rank
   - Max Rank: 2
   - Effect: Detect magic within 60 feet per rank (120 ft at rank 2); cast at will
   - Prerequisite: None
   - Notes: Utility divination ability

4. **Dark Patronage**
   - Cost: 1 point
   - Max Rank: 1
   - Effect: Gain +2 to all saving throws
   - Prerequisite: None
   - Notes: Represents patron's interest in keeping you alive

---

### Tier 2 Perks (2 points each)

5. **Dark One's Blessing II**
   - Cost: 2 points per rank
   - Max Rank: 2
   - Effect: Additional +5 temporary HP per rank when Dark One's Blessing triggers
   - Prerequisite: Dark One's Blessing I (max)
   - Notes: Total of +7 temporary HP per kill at maximum

6. **Dark One's Own Luck**
   - Cost: 2 points per rank
   - Max Rank: 2
   - Effect: Once per 10 minutes per rank, add d8 to a skill check (including crafting ot harvesting checks). Needs the creation of an ACMD command
   - Prerequisite: Dark Patronage
   - Notes: Tactical luck manipulation

7. **Fiendish Vigor**
   - Cost: 2 points
   - Max Rank: 1
   - Effect: Cast False Life on yourself at will without expending spell slots (1d4+4 temporary HP)
   - Prerequisite: Dark One's Blessing I (2 ranks)
   - Notes: Passive healing ability

8. **Pact Spell Amplification**
   - Cost: 2 points
   - Max Rank: 1
   - Effect: +2 to spell level when casting warlock spells
   - Prerequisite: Pact Connection (2 ranks)
   - Notes: Allows upcast spells without additional resource cost

---

### Tier 3 Perks (3-4 points each)

9. **Fiendish Resilience**
   - Cost: 3 points per rank
   - Max Rank: 2
   - Effect: Every ten minutes specify a damage type. Gain 25% resistance per rank for 1 minute. Need a ACMD command, and put code in compute_damtype_resistance
   - Prerequisite: Fiendish Vigor, Dark One's Blessing II (1 rank)
   - Notes: Flexible damage mitigation, stacks with other resistances

10. **Pact Soul**
    - Cost: 4 points
    - Max Rank: 1
    - Effect: +2 to charisma bonus
    - Prerequisite: Pact Connection (max), Dark Patronage
    - Notes: Comprehensive negotiation/manipulation enhancement

11. **Eldritch Endurance**
    - Cost: 3 points per rank
    - Max Rank: 2
    - Effect: +2 to concentration skill per rank
    - Prerequisite: Dark One's Blessing II (max)
    - Notes: Representation of patron strengthening your body

12. **Dark Revelation**
    - Cost: 3 points
    - Max Rank: 1
    - Effect: Your patron grants you insight into your enemies. When examining (using lore on) a creature, reveal its damage resistances, vulnerabilities, and immunities. Once per combat, gain +2 to hit and +2d6 damage against a target whose weaknesses you've identified.
    - Prerequisite: Eldritch Sight (max)
    - Notes: Strategic combat ability, patron provides knowledge of enemy weaknesses

---

### Tier 4 Capstone Perks (5+ points each)

13. **One with Patron**
    - Cost: 5 points
    - Max Rank: 1
    - Effect: Dark One's Blessings trigger at half threshold (50%) instead of 0 HP kills; gain +5 temp HP per trigger
    - Prerequisite: Dark One's Blessing II (max), Pact Soul
    - Notes: Ultimate healing/sustainability from patron relationship

14. **Hurl Through Hell**
    - Cost: 5 points
    - Max Rank: 1
    - Effect: Ability to cast at-will a spell that sends the creature to the abyss and back in an instant, dealing 5d10+level mental damage.
    - Prerequisite: Fiendish Resilience (1 rank), Pact Spell Amplification
    - Notes: Powerful control/damage ability, represents patron's domain

15. **Planar Anchor**
    - Cost: 6 points
    - Max Rank: 1
    - Effect: Gain a new spell that protects from being summoned or teleported to usable at-will on self or others. Add this spell to the revoke list.
    - Prerequisite: Hurl Through Hell, One with Patron
    - Notes: Represents anchoring to patron's realm

16. **Dark Master's Will**
    - Cost: 5 points
    - Max Rank: 1
    - Effect: Once per 5 minutes, when a will save fails, automatically succeed.
    - Prerequisite: Dark One's Own Luck (max), Pact Soul
    - Notes: Ultimate save manipulation from patron intervention

**Tree 2 Total: 42 points possible**

## Design Notes

### Balance Considerations
- **Eldritch Blast Damage**: Maximum of +10 damage (Tiers 1-3) represents significant but not overwhelming enhancement
- **Spell DCs**: Maximum +8 from Charisma and Pact perks, balanced against other casters
- **Damage Resistance**: Stack separately from other sources, limited by finite number of damage types
- **Healing/Sustainability**: Dark One's Blessing encourages aggressive play while providing meaningful survival option
- **Invocation Flexibility**: Multiple at-will spell-like abilities provide utility without breaking action economy

### Synergies Between Trees
1. **Eldritch Mastery + Pact Bonding**: Aggressive Eldritch Blast playstyle with healing sustain
2. **Eldritch Mastery + Invocation Mastery**: Enhanced blast with crowd control invocations
3. **Pact Bonding + Arcane Knowledge**: Defensive patron connection with spellcasting power
4. **Arcane Knowledge + Invocation Mastery**: Spell-heavy warlock with diverse magical toolkit
5. **All Trees**: Warlock can specialize in any tree or distribute evenly for versatility

### Key Code Integration Points
- Eldritch Blast function (magic.c) - damage calculation, invocation triggers
- Spell DC system (savingthrow_full in magic.c) - charisma-based saving throws
- Spell slot management (spell_prep.c) - warlock unique slot recovery
- Dark One's Blessing system (new function, called after creature death)
- Invocation effects (spell_parser.c, various invocation implementations)
- Pact selection (character creation/build system)
- Spell resistance calculations (mag_resistance in magic.c)

### Implementation Priority
1. **Phase 1**: Eldritch Blast damage bonuses and accuracy (Eldritch Mastery Tier 1-2)
2. **Phase 2**: Dark One's Blessing and Pact healing (Pact Bonding Tier 1-2)
3. **Phase 3**: Spell DC and penetration bonuses (Arcane Knowledge Tier 1-2)
4. **Phase 4**: Invocation implementation and at-will effects (Invocation Mastery Tier 1-2)
5. **Phase 5**: Advanced blast forms and invocations (all trees Tier 3)
6. **Phase 6**: Capstone abilities and special mechanics (all trees Tier 4)

### Perk Point Economy
- **Tier 1**: 16 perks × average 2 ranks = ~32 points available
- **Tier 2**: 16 perks × average 1.5 ranks = ~24 points available
- **Tier 3**: 16 perks × 1 rank = ~48 points available
- **Tier 4**: 12 perks × 1 rank = ~66 points available
- **Total**: 132 points available, players earn ~90 points = ~68% acquisition rate

Players will need to specialize to reach tier 4 capstones in any tree.

## Flavor Integration

### Warlock Identity
These perks reinforce the warlock's core identity as a pact-maker and forbidden knowledge seeker:
- **Eldritch Mastery** emphasizes the signature Eldritch Blast as a devastating focus weapon
- **Pact Bonding** represents deepening connection to a powerful, potentially dangerous patron
- **Invocation Mastery** showcases learning fragments of forbidden magic that bend reality
- **Arcane Knowledge** proves the warlock's intellectual pursuit of cosmic secrets

### Patron Relationships
Perks reflect different patron interactions:
- Dark One's Own Luck shows the patron subtly influencing fate
- Hurl Through Hell demonstrates the patron's domain over infernal realms
- Patron's Question and Eldritch Master represent direct patron communication
- Fiendish Resilience shows the patron altering the warlock's body
